Alone Not Alone Installation

2017

This installation project includes a glove and a scarf. Imagine a glove will hold your hand and a scarf will give you a hug when you feel... hmm... lonely and sad.

This experiment is about DIY the affection you need for socially awkward and introvert people. Next step: Add the fabric to the robotic hand, and, of course, make the scarf.
