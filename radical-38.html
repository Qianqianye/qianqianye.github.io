      女: Radical 38

      2019

      '女: Radical 38' is a WIP project, investing sexism in Chinese characters. '女‘ as a radical means woman and female in Mandarin. There are 681 characters to be found under this radical. I am shocked by how sexist some of the characters are, for example, 奴(slave), 奸(traitor), 妒(envy), etc.

      I am working on reconstructing these sexist characters and creating alternative narrative about Radical 38. More coming soon.

      Radical 38 Symbol Study


      怒 [nù] Rage & [女 nǚ] Female
      I have been doing research about Female Rage recently. Below are two surprising but pleasant learnings:
      1. In Mandarin, the pronunciations of these two words (nù & nǚ) are quite similar.
      2. The character 怒 (rage) is formed by three parts: 女(female), 又(again) & 心(heart), which means at old time, people might think ' rage = female heart is touched again'
