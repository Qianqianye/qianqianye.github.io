      PhobiaVR

      2017

      'PhobiaVR' is an on-going VR App Design.

      This app is designed for people who are suffering from or curious about phobia. In this app, users can get tailored virtual reality exposure therapy which is gradual and controlled. The ' calibrate - therapy - feedback' loop is the basic model of this VR UX design.
