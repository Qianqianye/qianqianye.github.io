P5.js Shan Shui

2018

I translated a small part of examples of p5.js documentation to Chinese in 2018. My work 'Shan Shui II' was featured as the homepage of p5js.org when it launched its Chinese version.

You can find the entire Chinese Translation for p5.js project at this Medium post by 2018 Processing Foundation Fellow Kenneth Lim.
