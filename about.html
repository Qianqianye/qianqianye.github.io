      About

      Qianqian Ye founded YE/S in 2016, focusing on UX design for architectural, digital and virtual spaces.

      Qianqian is an architect / designer / urbanist / coder / cosmologist. With a background in architecture, landscape as well as sociology, she finds it interesting to be a coder who designs and a designer who codes. She currently lives with       two cats in San Francisco, CA.

      She worked at a number of practices internationally, from China, the US, Denmark to Brazil, most recently including an abandoned landfill restoration project in Rio de Janeiro. Qianqian holds her Master degree in Landscape Architecture from Cornell University with a minor in Real Estate.
