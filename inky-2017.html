      Ink Painting 2017

      2017

      I started exploring ink from a young age via the study of tradition Chinese calligraphy. I use it as a means to distill energy. This medium offers simultaneous clarity and obscurity balanced by brush technique.

      The minimal nature of the medium offers a contrast to the complexity of the topic.
